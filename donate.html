Support Our Animals

Every dollar goes directly to the animals in our care — no admin fees, no overhead. Just love and medicine.

We run entirely on community support.

Hellena Animal Rescue is a 100% volunteer-run organization based in Houston, Texas. We rescue dogs and cats from shelters, streets, and crisis situations — providing medical care, safe foster homes, and love until they find their forever family.

We receive no government funding. Everything we do is made possible by people like you. Your donation — whether one-time or monthly — directly funds the animals in our care right now.

Donations are tax deductible. We are a registered 501(c)(3) tax exempt charity.

  • 🏥
    Veterinary CareExams, vaccinations, spay/neuter, and emergency treatment for every animal before adoption.
  • 🍖
    Food & NutritionQuality food and supplements to keep animals healthy while they wait for their forever home.
  • 🏠
    Safe ShelterClean, comfortable foster homes and kennel care for animals in crisis.
