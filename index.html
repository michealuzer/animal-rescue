Who We Are

Dedicated to Rescuing Animals in Crisis

Hellena Animal Rescue is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing and assisting animals in crisis across the Houston, Texas area. We serve as a resource and advocate for shelters, individuals, and animal control — fighting for any animal in need.

Many of the animals we rescue are available for adoption. You can also contribute to our cause through donations, or by joining our group of dedicated volunteers. 100% of donations go directly to the animals.

Simple Process

How Adoption Works

Adopting a rescue animal is easy and rewarding. Here's how to get started.

1
📝

Submit an Application

Browse our available animals and fill out an adoption application. We review every application carefully.

2
✅

Application Review

Our team reviews your application and notifies you of the outcome within 2–3 business days.

3
📄

Agreement & Fee

If accepted, we send you an adoption agreement to sign. Once signed, you pay the adoption fee to secure your pet.

4
🏡

Welcome Home!

With everything complete, your new companion is officially yours. We're always here to support you.
